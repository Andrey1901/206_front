La Playa Beach Resort Taba*****
14 KM Taba - Nuweiba Road Taba, South Sinai, Egypt

About the La Playa Beach Resort Taba:

Overlooking the magnificent view of the red-Sea on the Gulf of Aqaba, Le Playa Beach Resort Taba comprises 440 elegant guest rooms and suites with private terrace. It offers 2 outdoor pools, a private beach, a Turkish bath and a gym.

Each modern room is air-conditioned. Panoramic views of the Gulf of Aqaba can be seen from the balcony in some rooms. All luxurious rooms have a balcony or terrace.

Guests can relax at the hotel’s health club with a massage. Le Playa Beach Resort facilities include a tennis court, a hot tub and a diving and water sports centre.

The hotel offers 2 a la carte restaurants with seafood and Italian specialties by the beach as well as 2 buffet restaurants. Drinks and refreshments are served in all of Le Playa's 6 bars.
