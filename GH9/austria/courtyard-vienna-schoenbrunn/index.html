About the Courtyard by Marriott:

This newly built and state of the art Courtyard by Marriott hotel is ideally located just around the corner from Vienna's tourist attraction no. 1, Schloss Schoenbrunn. and easily reachable by car from highway A1 and A2. Modern architecture, quality textures and warm colors make our air-conditioned guest rooms comfortable and cozy. Ideal for the business traveller (big desk, ergonomic chair, high speed internet access, flat TV, tea/coffee making facilities, business corner, conference rooms, mini gym, garage) the hotel is also the place to start your sight seeing tour through Vienna. Explore Schoenbrunn and stroll through its lush gardens just like Sissi did. Your children's favorite place will be the world renown zoo. The subway located right next to us will take you to the city center within 10 minutes, where history and tradition but also the bustling life of a world metropolis are waiting for you. Whatever your reason for a trip to Vienna might be, we will make your stay with us an unforgettable one.

Schönbrunn for Children

One thing is certain — your kids will be crazy about Schönbrunn!

At the Children's Museum they can play with toys from imperial times, dress up as prince or princess, and learn about the world the imperial children lived in. Strolling through the Maze or romping around the Labyrinth and the Labyrinthikon Playground is great fun, too.

A very special highlight is the Vienna Zoo with Treetop Trail, Rainforest House with daily tropical thunderstorms, and more than 500 animals, from Giant Pandas and Polar Bears to Lions and Siberian Tigers. You also shouldn't miss the Desert House and the Palm House adjacent to the zoo. The Schönbrunn Panorama Train is the perfect way to explore all attractions with small or tired children.

By the way, the smoke-free Courtyard Vienna Schoenbrunn hotel offers spacious, family-friendly rooms, and a kid's menu in our restaurant.
