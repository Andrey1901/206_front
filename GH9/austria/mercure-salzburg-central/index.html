Mercure Salzburg Central
20 Sterneckstrasse - Salzburg - 5020 Salzburg, Austria

About the Mercure Salzburg Central:

The Mercure Salzburg Central is easy to reach by car or public transport, with Getreidegasse, Mirabell Garden, the historical center, Linzergasse and the main train station just a few minutes' walk away. This 4-star hotel in the city of Mozart is ideally located for cultural trips, city breaks and short stays. The conference and seminar hotel is very highly rated among business guests thanks to its proximity to the conference center, exhibition center and airport.

Standard Twin Room

Standard Double Room in a classic style with twin beds. With bathtub, flat-screen TV, minibar, free WIFI and free tea & coffee-making facilities.

Superior Double Room

Superior Room (minimum 258.3 sq. ft [24 m²]) with modern decor and king size bed or twin beds. Equipped with walk in shower, flat-screen TV, minibar, laptop safe, free WIFI and free tea & coffee-making facilities.

Privilege Double Room

Modern Privilege Rooms with king-size beds. The rooms come with a flat-screen TV, free minibar, free WIFI, laptop safe, Nespresso coffee machine and tea-making facilities, walk-in rainshower, VIP bathroom fittings and free late check-out up to 2pm.

Suite with Living and Sleeping Areas

Modern luxurious suites from 484 sq. ft. (45 m²) to 624 sq. ft. (58 m²) offer space to relax. The rooms come with a flat-screen TV, minibar, free WIFI, VIP welcome, laptop safe, Nespresso coffee machine, tea-making facilities and VIP bathroom fittings.
