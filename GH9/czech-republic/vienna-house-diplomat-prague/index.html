About the Vienna House Diplomat:

Located near the Prague Castle, in green and friendly surroundings in the diplomatic quarter, the four star Golden Tulip Hotel Diplomat can easily be reached from the city centre by metro or in 10 minute driving by car from the airport. The Relax Centre is situated in the 9th floor with a magnificant view on the Prague Castle. Whirlpool, Fitness, Solarium Sauna & Massage.

After a tiresome day, spent at a business meeting or sightseeing, our guests can use one of Diplomat’s relaxation facilities.

The four-star Golden Tulip Diplomat Hotel**** Prague is located on the very edge of Prague’s historic centre, it takes Only * 10 minutes to get there from the City Airport. Close to the Prague Caste, it is surrounded mainly by diplomatic residences.

RELAX AND ENJOY THE VIEW

Whether you are on business or touring the city, a day spent taking in the sights of Prague can be tiring. So let yourself unwind in the Diplomat Health Club and enjoy the breathtaking views of Prague Castle whilst you relax in the Wellness area. Guests receive complimentary access to the Health Club fitness centre. For a fee, you can book private spa, saunas, steam rooms, jacuzzi and massage treatments.
