About the Grand Hotel Moderne:

The Grand Hotel Moderne was built in 1896 by the family of Bernadette Soubirous, it stands just 100 meters from the shrine of Notre Dame in the center of the historic pilgrims' destination of Lourdes.

Standard room amenities include climate control, hairdryers, and direct-dial phones. Guests can dine in the Art Nouveau dining room, with its imposing pillars, mirrors and chandeliers, which serves both French and Italian dishes and is open daily for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The hotel's onsite store sells a range of religious items as well as souvenirs, and guest can seek help and advice on restaurants and trips further afield from the concierge.

Outside Lourdes, guests can visit the Bétharram caves (15 kilometers), the Pyrenees mountains (40 kilometers), or take a trip to the coast at Biarritz (160 kilometers). Tarbes-Ossun-Lourdes Airport is 20 minutes and 12 kilometers away

Rooms & Suites

Recently renovated, the Grand Hotel Moderne combines the best of old world charm with modern comforts.

The hotel has 106 rooms including 5 suites. Every room and suite is a spacious, peaceful oasis of calm, providing guests with an enjoyable and comfortable stay.

Designed and furnished with classic French Louis XVI style furniture, large and comfortable beds, and new bathrooms appointed in Italian marble. Most rooms also have a private balcony.

Classic Rooms

Elegantly appointed classic rooms are single, double, twin, or triple (2 beds) room configurations.

Superior Rooms

With balconies providing side views of the Esplanade where the candlelight procession takes place, these elegantly appointed superior rooms are double, twin, triple (2 beds) or quadruple (2 beds) room configurations.

Master Suites

With magnificent 180 degree views of the nightly candlelight procession, our master suites offer the best experience of the evening’s ceremony without leaving the comfort of the hotel. Master suites have a large bedroom with a king-sized bed or two single beds, a bright and spacious living room, and a large balcony with sweeping views of the entire Esplanade.

Family Rooms

Ideal for the family, these elegantly appointed family rooms feature two bedrooms and an en-suite bathroom for triple or quadruple occupancy. All have a balcony with views.

Disabled Access

The Grand Hotel Moderne offers accessible accommodation for elderly and disabled guests:

  • Entrance to the hotel includes a street-level driveway where cars can temporarily park to embark and disembark guests, their luggage and mobility equipment.
  • The street-level entrance is fitted with two automatic sliding doors that opens widely into a level reception area.
  • A ramp leads to the restaurant and bar area for full wheelchair access.
  • Two wheelchair accessible lifts provide access to all levels of the hotel.
  • All entrances (including all hotel guest room entrances) are wide enough for full wheelchair access.

Grand Hotel Moderne has four extra-spacious rooms equipped with wet rooms. These oversized bathrooms include a wheelchair friendly roll-in shower, shower chairs, grab rails, and raised toilet seats. The rooms are elegantly appointed, bright and spacious for wheelchairs, with large comfortable beds and views of the Esplanade and street.
