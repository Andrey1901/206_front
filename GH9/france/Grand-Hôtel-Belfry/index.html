About the Grand Hôtel Belfry:

An institution in Lourdes, the “Grand Hotel de la Grotte” has just changed owners. It is now called the “Grand Hotel BELFRY”. Ideally located at the foot of the fortified castle of Lourdes, in a green setting, close to the sanctuaries, the team welcomes you in a contemporary atmosphere 24/7.

The new owners, Mr. and Mrs. LEGUIDE want to give back to this former 5 stars its prestige of yesteryear. In 2017, it experienced a complete renovation unprecedented in its history. Metamorphosed into a modern hotel, it is now inspired by design, contemporary furniture and soft colors.

The Grand Hôtel Belfry offers 83 guestrooms. The apartments, suites, junior suites or rooms are decorated in Napoleon III style. They are equipped with digital satellite TV, a digital safe, mini bar, Internet access, direct dial phone, air-conditioning and with a modern bathroom. You will relax in a friendly environment and will enjoy the luxury and comfort of the hotel.

Restaurant - Table de Fébus

Breakfast starts from 6 am to 11 am and can be taken in your room or in the dining room lined with large windows, with a view on the Gave de Pau. The variety of the buffet is composed exclusively of gourmet and artisanal products, thanks to our carefully selected local partners, which make it a special moment.

The bar is open 24/7 for a glass of Veuve Cliquot champagne, a large whiskey, a fruit juice Alain Miliat accompanied by first choice tapas (Iberian ham, "Le Borvo" salmon, whole foie gras, guinea fowl terrines, tuna toastines, ...)

The tea room offers you the discovery of 20 great Dammann teas, a selection of excellent coffees or our famous hot chocolate "L'africain".

The 24/7 room service offers tastings of dishes prepared by Michelin-starred chefs such as Emmanuel Renaut ***, Gilles Goujon ***, and Jean-Michel Lorain **, as well as desserts prepared by great pastry chefs such as Frédéric Bau, Philippe Conticini or Vincent Ferniot.
