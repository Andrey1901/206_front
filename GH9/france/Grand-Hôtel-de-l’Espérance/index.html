Grand Hôtel de l’Espérance ***
16, boulevard Sainte-Anne, 14100 Lisieux, France

The Grand Hôtel de l’Espérance is ideally situated to discover the religious heritage of Lisieux.

The Grand Hôtel de l’Espérance with its Anglo-Norman style front sits proudly in the heart of the town centre Behind its half timbered walls and little balconies you will find a traditional French hotel with warm & attentive hospitality.

The Grand Hôtel de l’Espérance is recommended by the Michelin guide & the Bottin Gourmand
